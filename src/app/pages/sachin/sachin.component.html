CA. Sachin Jain

Biography

Sachin is an Associate member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) since 2015 with more than 5 years of experience working extensively in finance sector. He is a Certified Concurrent Auditor of Banks since 2018. A passionate and adept financial expert, prior to being a partner at the firm, Sachin worked as a Branch credit manager at Reliance Capital Ltd., Indore, a that time leading NBFC of India. His key responsibilities were loan underwriting, business analytics, customer interaction & branch upkeep. He gained an expertise of the business practices observed at such institutions thereby developing a sound understanding of their environment and the concerns involved. In 2017 he joined the firm and since then has been one of the co-founder of the said partnership.
